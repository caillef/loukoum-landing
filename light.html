What is Loukoum Light ?

A browser extension that allows you to check the latest news in a single click from TradingView, CoinMarketCap, Binance and the major exchanges.
More than 1000 sources gathered continuously :
Official Subreddits
Official Twitter accounts
RSS feeds
Not a magic algorithm but more a new way to D.Y.O.R (Do Your Own Research) without spending hours daily checking hundreds of different websites. The score is defined by the number of shares.
